Fort Worth vs Dallas: Casino Access

How Fort Worth's location affects your casino options.

Fort Worth Advantage for WinStar

Fort Worth is actually positioned slightly better than Dallas for reaching WinStar. The I-35W corridor from Fort Worth through Denton to the border avoids the worst of Dallas traffic. On a clear day, you can make the drive in just over an hour.

Dallas Advantage for Choctaw

For Choctaw Durant, Dallas has the edge since US-75 runs north from downtown Dallas. From Fort Worth, you'll need to cross the Metroplex first. Still, 90 minutes is very manageable.

Day Trip or Overnight?

Both WinStar and Choctaw are easily day-trippable from Fort Worth. For the full experience, an overnight stay lets you enjoy the hotels, dining, and entertainment without worrying about the drive home. Weekend packages at both properties often include free play credits.

💡 Pro Tip: Leave Fort Worth early on a Saturday morning to beat traffic on I-35W. The Denton stretch can get busy by mid-morning. Alternatively, go on a weekday for smaller crowds and better hotel rates at both casinos.
