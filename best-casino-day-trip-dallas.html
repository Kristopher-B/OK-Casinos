Day Trip Tips

How to Maximize Your
Dallas Casino Day Trip

When to Leave

Ideal departure: 9–10 AM. This gets you to WinStar by 10–11 AM with the whole day ahead. Avoid leaving Friday afternoon — I-35 northbound gets packed with weekend casino traffic from DFW. Saturday morning is a great departure time. Sunday is the quietest day at the casino if you prefer a more relaxed floor.

When to Head Home

Ideal return: 7–9 PM. This gives you 8–10 hours at the casino and gets you home at a reasonable hour. The drive south on I-35 is typically clear in the evening. Don't stay past midnight unless you're prepared for a late drive — drowsy driving is dangerous.

Bring Cash

Casino ATMs charge $5–$8 per transaction. Your bank may add fees too. Withdraw cash from your own bank before leaving Dallas. Decide your gambling budget before you arrive and bring exactly that amount — no more. Leave credit cards and extra cash in the car's locked glove box.

Eat Smart

Both WinStar and Choctaw have 20+ dining options, but casino restaurants can be expensive. Eat a good breakfast before you leave Dallas. Plan one sit-down meal at the casino and supplement with the food court or buffet. Your rewards card earns dining points too — always present it.

The I-35 Traffic Reality

I-35 between Dallas and the Oklahoma border is a two-lane highway in spots and can bottleneck, especially on Friday evenings and Sunday afternoons. If you can travel outside these windows, do it. Waze or Google Maps can help find faster routes when traffic is heavy. The US-75 route to Choctaw Durant is often less congested.

Important: Oklahoma law requires you to be 18+ to enter most casino gaming floors (some areas require 21+). Bring a valid photo ID — you will be checked. Minors cannot be left unattended in vehicles.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the closest casino to Dallas, Texas?
WinStar World Casino in Thackerville, OK — approximately 75 miles (~1 hour) north on I-35. It's the first casino you hit crossing into Oklahoma. Choctaw Durant is second at about 95 miles (~1hr 20min).
How long is the drive from Dallas to WinStar?
About 1 hour from downtown Dallas via I-35 North. From the northern suburbs (Frisco, Plano, McKinney), around 45–50 minutes. Friday evening traffic on I-35 can add 15–30 minutes.
Is a day trip from Dallas to WinStar worth it?
Yes. The 1-hour drive is manageable, and WinStar has the world's largest gaming floor (10,500+ games). Leave by 10 AM, play all day, and be home by 10 PM. Thousands of Texans do this every weekend.
Should I go to WinStar or Choctaw from Dallas?
For a day trip, WinStar — it's 20 miles closer on I-35. For an overnight, Choctaw Durant's AAA Four Diamond hotel and The District make it the better resort experience, worth the extra 20 minutes.
Can I visit both in one day from Dallas?
Technically yes, but ambitious. They're 40 miles apart (45 min drive). You'd spend 3.5+ hours driving total. Better as a weekend trip — stay overnight at one and day-trip to the other.
