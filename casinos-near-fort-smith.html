Arkansas Visitor Guide

Fort Smith to Oklahoma — What to Know

Fort Smith, Arkansas has some of the best casino access of any city in the state — simply because it sits right on the Oklahoma border. Cherokee Casino Roland is literally a 15-minute drive west on I-40, making it one of the most convenient casino trips you can take.

Cherokee Roland — The Quick Trip

Cherokee Casino Roland is a Cherokee Nation property with a solid gaming floor including slots, table games, and one of the better poker rooms in eastern Oklahoma. It's perfect for an evening out — no planning or overnight stay required. Cross the state line, play, and be home in time for the late news.

Choctaw Pocola — The Alternative

Choctaw Casino in Pocola is another close option at about 20 minutes south. It's a smaller Choctaw Nation property with electronic games and dining. Worth knowing about if Roland is crowded or you want a change of scenery.

💡 Pro Tip: If you're a poker player, Cherokee Roland's poker room runs regular tournaments and cash games. Check their schedule before you go — they host some of the best poker action in eastern Oklahoma.

Weekend Trips

For a bigger casino experience, Hard Rock Tulsa is about 1 hour 45 minutes northwest via the Muskogee Turnpike. It's a full resort with a hotel, golf course, and Hard Rock Live concert venue. Downstream Casino Resort (2.5 hours north) is another solid weekend option with a hotel and regular entertainment.
