Travel Tips

Kansas City to Oklahoma — What to Know

Downstream Casino Resort is the clear pick for Kansas City residents looking for the closest Oklahoma casino experience. Located at the exact corner where Oklahoma, Kansas, and Missouri converge, it's designed to serve visitors from all three states.

The Drive

Take I-49 south from Kansas City to Joplin, then follow US-69/US-60 to the Quapaw area. The drive is mostly highway and straightforward — about 3 hours in normal traffic. If you're planning an overnight trip, Downstream has a full-service hotel on-site.

What to Expect

Downstream offers a complete resort experience: table games, over 2,000 gaming machines, multiple restaurants, a hotel, and regular concert events. It's a solid destination for a weekend getaway from KC.

💡 Pro Tip: Sign up for Downstream's players club before you start playing. You'll earn points on every dollar that convert to free play and dining credits — and the signup bonus alone makes it worthwhile.

Worth the Extra Drive?

If you have more time, Hard Rock Tulsa (4 hours) offers a larger gaming floor with 2,600+ slots, poker room, golf course, and major concerts at Hard Rock Live. It's worth the extra hour if you're planning a full weekend.
