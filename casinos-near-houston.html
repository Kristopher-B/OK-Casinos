Houston Visitors

Planning Your Casino Trip

Make the most of a longer drive from Houston to Oklahoma's casinos.

Make It a Weekend

From Houston, Oklahoma casinos are a weekend trip rather than a day trip. The good news: both WinStar and Choctaw Durant have full-service hotels, so you can check in, gamble, dine, and enjoy entertainment without leaving the resort.

Best Route from Houston

Take I-45 North to Dallas (about 4 hours). From there, WinStar is just one more hour north on I-35, and Choctaw Durant is 90 minutes northeast on US-75. Consider stopping in Dallas for lunch or dinner to break up the drive.

Hit Two Casinos in One Trip

Since WinStar and Choctaw Durant are only about 45 minutes apart, you can visit both in a single weekend trip. Stay Friday night at one, Saturday night at the other. That's the optimal Houston-to-Oklahoma casino strategy.

💡 Pro Tip: Book hotel rooms midweek for the best rates. Both WinStar and Choctaw offer weekday packages that can include free play credits, making the trip even more worthwhile for Houston visitors.
Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What is the closest casino to Houston?
WinStar World Casino in Oklahoma is about 290 miles / 4.5 hours north. Within Texas, the Kickapoo Lucky Eagle Casino in Eagle Pass is about 280 miles southwest, but Oklahoma's casinos offer far more gaming options.
Is it worth driving from Houston to Oklahoma for casinos?
Yes — if you plan it as a weekend trip. WinStar is the world's largest casino and Choctaw Durant is AAA Four Diamond rated. Both have hotels, dining, and entertainment that make a multi-day trip worthwhile.
Can I fly from Houston to Oklahoma casinos?
You can fly Houston to Dallas (1 hour) and then rent a car or use a shuttle to WinStar or Choctaw. Some casino packages include transportation from DFW. Direct flights to Oklahoma City are also available if visiting Riverwind.
Should I visit WinStar or Choctaw from Houston?
Both are excellent. WinStar is slightly closer and is the world's largest casino. Choctaw Durant has a higher-rated hotel and resort experience. Best strategy: visit both in one weekend since they're only 45 minutes apart.
