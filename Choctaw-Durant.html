Slot Machines

With 7,400+ electronic gaming machines, Choctaw Durant has one of the largest slot floors in the United States. You'll find everything from penny machines to high-limit rooms, the latest video slots from IGT, Aristocrat, and Konami, and a dedicated high-limit area for players who prefer larger bets and more privacy.

The floor is laid out across multiple connected wings of the resort, so it never feels overwhelming even at peak hours on weekends. Signs are clear and staff are everywhere to help if you get lost.

💡 Insider Tip

The high-limit room has better odds on many machines and significantly less noise. If you're planning to spend $100+ in a session, it's worth checking out even if you're not a regular high roller.

Table Games

Over 100 table games spread across Blackjack, Craps, Roulette, Baccarat, Three Card Poker, Ultimate Texas Hold'em, and more. Minimums vary by time of day — weekday afternoons you can often find $5 and $10 minimums. Friday nights and weekends push most tables to $15–$25 minimum.

⚠️ Know Before You Go

Table game minimums jump significantly on Friday evening and Saturday. If you want to play at lower stakes, aim for a weekday visit or arrive before 5pm on weekends.

Poker Room

Choctaw Durant has a dedicated poker room with cash games and tournaments. Texas Hold'em is the main game with multiple stakes available. The room runs daily tournaments — check the Choctaw website for the current tournament schedule before your trip.

On March 7, 2025, Choctaw Casino Resort Durant became the first brick-and-mortar casino in the United States to accept cryptocurrency for purchases. Powered by Bitline and Everi, guests can now use crypto at resort dining, retail, and hotel transactions.

Bitcoin (BTC) Ethereum (ETH) Tether (USDT) Circle (USDC)

This makes Choctaw Durant genuinely unique in the US gaming market. While online casinos and sportsbooks have accepted crypto for years, no physical casino had integrated crypto payments at the property level until this rollout. It's a significant story for casino tech watchers and crypto holders alike.

📌 Note

Cryptocurrency is currently accepted for resort purchases (hotel, dining, retail) — not directly on the gaming floor for chips or credits. This may expand over time. Confirm current accepted use cases with the property before your visit.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Is there an age requirement to enter?
You must be 18 or older to enter the casino floor. The hotel and some restaurants are open to all ages. The District entertainment complex (bowling, theater, arcade) is family-friendly.
Does Choctaw Durant have a dress code?
No strict dress code for the casino floor. Smart casual is appropriate for fine dining at Epoch. The rest of the property is relaxed — jeans and a clean shirt are totally fine.
Can I smoke inside?
Yes. Like most Oklahoma tribal casinos, smoking is permitted in designated areas on the gaming floor. Non-smoking sections are available. The hotel rooms are non-smoking.
Is alcohol served on the gaming floor?
Yes. Cocktail service is available on the gaming floor. Oklahoma tribal casinos are permitted to serve alcohol unlike many state-regulated facilities.
How do I earn free play?
Sign up for Choctaw Rewards (free). Every 1,000 points you earn converts to $1 in free play. Points come from slot play, table games, and — at Amber tier and above — hotel, dining, and retail spend.
Is Choctaw Durant worth a day trip from Dallas?
Absolutely. At 95 miles and about 80 minutes of driving, it's closer than many people realize. The scale of the property makes it a full-day destination — gaming, a show, a nice dinner, and you're back in Dallas by midnight.
What's better — Choctaw or WinStar?
It depends on what you want. WinStar has more gaming machines (10,500+ vs 7,400+) and two golf courses. Choctaw Durant has the AAA Four Diamond hotel rating, a better entertainment complex (The District), and accepted crypto first. We have a full comparison page that breaks it down.
